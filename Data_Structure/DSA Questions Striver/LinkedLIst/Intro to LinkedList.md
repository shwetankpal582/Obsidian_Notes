## Array to Linked List
Given an array of integer **arr.** Your task is to construct the linked list from arr & return the **head** of the linked list.
**Example:**

> [!information] Example
> **INPUT**: arr = [1,2,3,4,5]
> **OUTPUT**: LinkedList: $1-> 2-> 3-> 4-> 5$
